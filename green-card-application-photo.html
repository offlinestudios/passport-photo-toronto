Green Card Photo Requirements

The USCIS has specific requirements for Green Card application photos. Our service ensures your photos will comply with all these requirements:

  • 2x2 inches (51x51 mm) in size
  • Taken within the last 6 months to reflect your current appearance
  • Full face view directly facing the camera
  • Neutral facial expression or natural smile
  • Both eyes open and clearly visible
  • Plain white or off-white background
  • No glasses (as of 2016 USCIS regulations)
  • No hats or head coverings (except for religious or medical reasons)
  • Normal clothing (no uniforms or similar attire)
  • Proper lighting with no shadows on face or background

Green Card Application Information

While we specialize in Green Card photos, here's some general information about the application process:

  • Form I-485 is used to apply for lawful permanent resident status (Green Card)
  • Most Green Cards are valid for 10 years before renewal is required
  • Conditional Green Cards (marriage-based within 2 years) are valid for 2 years
  • Processing times vary significantly based on application type and USCIS workload
  • Two identical photos are typically required for the application

For the most current information about Green Card applications, please visit the official USCIS Green Card website.
