Schengen Visa Photo Requirements

The European Union has specific requirements for Schengen visa photos. Our service ensures your photos will comply with all these requirements:

  • Size: 35mm x 45mm (standard Schengen visa size)
  • Recent photograph taken within the last 6 months
  • Color photo with a light grey or off-white background
  • Full face view directly facing the camera
  • Neutral facial expression with mouth closed
  • Eyes open and clearly visible
  • No shadows on the face or background
  • No glasses (unless medically required)
  • No head coverings (except for religious reasons)
  • Head height between 70-80% of the photo's height
  • Proper lighting with no red-eye effect

Schengen Visa Application Information

While we specialize in visa photos, here's some general information about Schengen visa applications:

  • Schengen visa applications must be submitted to the embassy or consulate of your main destination country
  • Processing times vary by country, but typically take 10-15 working days
  • Standard Schengen visas are valid for up to 90 days within a 180-day period
  • Two identical photos are typically required for the application
  • Applications should be submitted at least 15 days before your planned trip

For the most current information about Schengen visa applications, please visit the official website of the embassy or consulate of your destination country.
