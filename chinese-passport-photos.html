Chinese Passport Photo Requirements

The Chinese government has specific requirements for passport photos. Our service ensures your photos will comply with all these requirements:

  • 33mm x 48mm in size
  • Taken within the last 6 months
  • Full face view directly facing the camera
  • Neutral facial expression (no smiling)
  • Both eyes open and clearly visible
  • Plain white background
  • No glasses (unless worn daily for medical reasons)
  • No hats or head coverings (except for religious purposes)
  • Normal clothing (no uniforms or similar attire)
  • Proper lighting with no shadows on face or background

Chinese Passport Application Information

While we specialize in passport photos, here's some general information about Chinese passport applications:

  • Chinese passports are typically valid for 10 years for adults
  • Chinese citizens in Canada can apply at the Chinese Embassy or Consulates
  • Processing time varies depending on the application type and location
  • Expedited processing may be available for urgent cases

For the most current information about Chinese passport applications, please visit the official Chinese Embassy in Canada website.
