School ID Photo Requirements

Most schools require standard passport-size photos for student IDs and records. Our service ensures your photos will comply with common requirements, including:

  • Standard passport size (2x2 inches or 51x51mm)
  • Recent photograph
  • Color photo with a white or light-colored background
  • Full face view directly facing the camera
  • Natural facial expression (slight smile is usually acceptable for school photos)
  • Eyes open and clearly visible
  • No shadows on the face or background
  • Proper lighting with no red-eye effect

School ID Photo Tips

To ensure the best results for your child's school ID photos, consider these tips:

  • Dress your child in solid-colored clothing (avoid patterns, logos, or bright colors)
  • Ensure hair is neat and does not cover the face or eyes
  • For children who wear glasses, consider removing them for the photo unless required
  • Practice a natural expression at home before coming to the studio
  • Schedule the photo session when your child is well-rested and in a good mood
  • Check with your school for any specific photo requirements before your session
