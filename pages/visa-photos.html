Common Visa Photo Requirements

While requirements vary by country, most visa photos must meet these general criteria:

  • Recent photo (usually taken within the last 6 months)
  • Clear, sharp image with no shadows
  • Plain white or light-colored background
  • Neutral facial expression
  • Both ears visible (in most cases)
  • No glasses (for most countries)
  • No head coverings (except for religious purposes)
  • Proper lighting with no red-eye or flash reflection
