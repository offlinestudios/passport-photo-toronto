About Our Location

Passport Photo Toronto is conveniently located in the heart of downtown Toronto, just steps away from OCAD University and the Art Gallery of Ontario. Our studio is easily accessible by public transit, with St. Patrick subway station just a short walk away.

We're situated in a professional photography studio environment, ensuring high-quality photos with proper lighting and equipment. Our central location makes us accessible from all parts of Toronto, making it easy to get your passport photos taken during your lunch break or while running errands downtown.

Getting Here

By TTC

St. Patrick Station (Line 1) is a 5-minute walk away. Osgoode Station (Line 1) is also nearby, about 7 minutes walking distance.

By Car

Limited street parking is available on McCaul St. Several paid parking lots are located nearby, including one at 105 McCaul St and another at 60 Queen St W.

By Bike

Bike racks are available on McCaul St. We're also close to several Bike Share Toronto stations.
