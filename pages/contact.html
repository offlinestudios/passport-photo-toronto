Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need an appointment?

No appointment is necessary. Simply walk in during our business hours, and we'll serve you on a first-come, first-served basis. Most customers are in and out in less than 10 minutes.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), debit cards, and cash payments.

How long does it take to get my photos?

Your photos will be ready within minutes of your session. We process and print your photos on-site while you wait. Digital copies, if requested, are typically emailed within 1 hour.

Is parking available nearby?

There is metered street parking available on McCaul Street and surrounding areas. Several public parking lots are also located within a short walking distance. We're also easily accessible by public transit.
